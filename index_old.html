What is Machine Learning?

What They Say:

Arthur Samuel (1959): Field of study that gives computers the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed.

Tom Mitchell (1998): A computer program is said to learn from experience E with respect to some task T and some performance measure P, if its performance on T, as measured by P, improves with experience E.

What I Say:

Machine Learning means exactly what itself indicate: machine learns from data, understands the pattern and make prediction or decision.

Example:

Samll kids can determine whether an animal is dog or cat easily by looking at the shape or color of the animal. Similarly, machine can also learn from a large amount of pictures of dogs and cats and then determine whether a new picture is a dog or cat.

Why Machine Learning?

Comapred to "human learning", machine learning is more efficient and accurate. Following the above example, machine can determine whether a million pictures are a dog or cat in a few seconds.
